About GNSS Site Manager

The GNSS site manager application has been developed by Geoscience Australia to view and edit GNSS site metadata.


All site information is publicly available, however, edit access is restricted to site owners and site metadata custodians. To apply for edit access complete the registration form. To provide information about metadata errors or provide feedback, please email the GNSS Informatics team at gnss@ga.gov.au and include your name, position and institutional affiliation.
